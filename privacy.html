Bustro Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 4, 2025

Simple Summary: Bustro uses your location to show nearby bus stops and collects anonymous usage analytics to improve the app. We don't collect personal information or track you across other apps.

1. Information We Collect

Location Information

  • What: Your device's location when you use the app
  • Why: To show nearby bus stops and calculate walking distances
  • When: Only when you open the app or use location features
  • Storage: Not stored on our servers - stays on your device

App Preferences

  • What: Your favorite bus stops, custom names, and chosen icons/colors
  • Why: To provide personalized experience
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud (if enabled)

Usage Analytics

  • What: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and error events
  • Why: To improve app performance and user experience
  • Examples: Bus stop views, menu interactions, rating prompts, user engagement
  • Service: Collected via Firebase Analytics (Google)

Crash and Error Reports

  • What: Technical information when the app crashes or encounters errors
  • Why: To identify and fix bugs for better app stability
  • Includes: Device model, iOS version, crash logs, error messages
  • Service: Firebase Crashlytics (Google)

2. Information We DON'T Collect

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Your location when app is closed
  • Location history or tracking
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Usage data from other apps

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Show bus stops near your current location
  • Calculate and display walking distances
  • Save your favorite stops and preferences
  • Provide map-based navigation features
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and performance
  • Generate crash reports to identify and fix technical issues

4. Data Sharing

We share limited technical data with service providers as described below.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates with:

  • Google Firebase: For analytics, crash reporting, and app configuration
  • Singapore LTA (Land Transport Authority): For real-time bus data (we don't send your location to them)
  • Apple Weather: For weather information (handled by iOS, not us)
  • Apple iCloud: For syncing your preferences and favorites (if enabled by you)
  • Map Services: When you choose to get directions (Apple Maps, Google Maps, etc.)

Data Shared with Google

Through Firebase services, the following data may be shared with Google:

  • App usage analytics and interaction events
  • Crash reports and technical error information
  • App configuration requests
  • No personal identification or location data is shared

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: User preferences and favorites stored on your iPhone and iCloud (if enabled)
  • Analytics Data: Usage patterns sent to Firebase Analytics for app improvement
  • Crash Reports: Technical error information sent to Firebase Crashlytics
  • iOS Security: Protected by Apple's built-in security features
  • App Deletion: All data is removed when you delete the app

6. Your Privacy Controls

Location Permissions

  • Control: You can disable location access anytime in Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Bustro
  • Impact: App will still work, but won't show nearby stops or distances

Data Deletion

  • Favorites: Delete individual favorites within the app
  • Local Data: Delete the app to remove all stored information
  • Analytics Data: Managed by Google's data retention policies (typically 2-14 months)
  • iCloud Data: Remove from your iCloud settings if you have sync enabled

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, as we don't collect personal information from anyone.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We'll notify users of significant changes through app updates. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: hi@bustro.app

10. Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Apple App Store Privacy Requirements

Your Privacy Matters

Bustro is designed with privacy in mind. We believe you should have control over your data, which is why we keep everything local to your device and don't track or share your information.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Live Bus Tracking and how do I use it?

Live Bus Tracking automatically monitors your selected bus arrivals with real-time updates, even when you minimize the app.

How to enable:

  1. Go to Settings > Preferences > Auto-Track Bus Arrivals (toggle on)
  2. When you view any bus stop details, tracking automatically begins
  3. You can also manually toggle tracking for specific stops

Benefits: Continuous monitoring, battery-optimized updates, and smart background refresh.

How do I customize my search radius for nearby stops?

Customize your search area:

  1. Open Settings from the main menu
  2. Go to Preferences
  3. Adjust "Search Radius" from 100m to 1000m
  4. Changes apply immediately to your nearby stops

Tip: Smaller radius (100-300m) for urban areas, larger radius (500-1000m) for suburban areas.

Why can't I see nearby bus stops?

Solution: Enable location permissions by going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Bustro and select "While Using App".

Additional checks:

  • Ensure your search radius isn't too small (check Settings > Preferences)
  • Try refreshing your location by pulling down on the nearby stops list
  • Check if you're in an area with bus stops (Singapore only)

Note: Bustro only uses your location when the app is open to show nearby stops and calculate walking distances.

Are the bus timings accurate?

Answer: Yes! Our data comes directly from Singapore's Land Transport Authority (LTA) DataMall API v3 in real-time. Timing may vary slightly due to traffic conditions, but we show the most current information available.

Data includes:

  • Vehicle type indicators (single deck, double deck, bendy buses)
  • Wheelchair accessibility status
  • Passenger load (seats available, standing room, limited standing)
  • Precise arrival predictions ("Arriving", "2 min", etc.)

Refresh rate: You can customize refresh intervals from 15 seconds to 2 minutes in Settings.

How do I add a bus stop to favorites?

Steps:

  1. Tap on any bus stop to open details
  2. Tap the heart icon (♡) to add to favorites
  3. Customize with a personal name, icon, and color
  4. Access quickly from the "Favorites" tab

Advanced features:

  • iCloud Sync: Favorites automatically sync across all your devices
  • Visual Organization: Choose custom colors and icons for easy identification
  • Reorder: Drag and drop to organize your favorites list
  • Map Integration: Favorites display with custom styling on the map

What does the weather widget do?

Feature: The weather widget uses Apple WeatherKit to show current conditions and help you plan your journey. Knowing if it's raining or sunny helps decide when to leave and what to bring.

Information displayed:

  • Current temperature and conditions
  • Hourly precipitation chances
  • Location-based weather (your current location or Singapore default)
  • Automatic 15-minute updates

Tip: Tap the weather widget to open the full Weather app for detailed forecasts and radar.

How do I access and use the MRT map?

Access: Tap the MRT button from the main interface to view Singapore's complete MRT network map.

Features:

  • Interactive Viewing: Pan and zoom to explore the entire network
  • High Resolution: Crystal clear map with all stations and lines
  • Legend: Comprehensive legend for all MRT lines and stations
  • Integration: Find bus stops near MRT stations for seamless transfers

Journey Planning: Perfect for planning multi-modal trips combining buses and trains across Singapore.

How does iCloud sync work with my favorites?

Automatic Sync: When enabled, your favorites, custom names, colors, and icons automatically sync across all your Apple devices.

Setup:

  1. Ensure you're signed into iCloud on all devices
  2. Favorites sync automatically in the background
  3. Changes appear within minutes on other devices

Troubleshooting sync issues:

  • Check iCloud settings in iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
  • Ensure you have internet connectivity
  • Try toggling airplane mode on/off to refresh sync

Troubleshooting

Live Bus Tracking not working or draining battery?

Common solutions:

  1. Check Settings: Ensure "Auto-Track Bus Arrivals" is enabled in Settings > Preferences
  2. Manual Toggle: Try manually enabling tracking for specific bus stops
  3. Background Refresh: Enable Background App Refresh for Bustro in iOS Settings
  4. Internet Connection: Tracking requires active internet connection

Battery Optimization:

  • Tracking is designed to be battery-efficient with smart refresh intervals
  • Consider reducing refresh rate in Settings if battery usage is a concern
  • Tracking automatically pauses when not needed

App is crashing or running slowly

Quick fixes:

  1. Force-close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Ensure you have the latest app version
  4. Check available storage space (need at least 1GB free)

Bus timing not updating

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection (WiFi or cellular)
  • Try switching between WiFi and cellular data
  • LTA occasionally has maintenance - timing will resume automatically

Map not loading properly

Troubleshooting:

  • Ensure location services are enabled
  • Check internet connectivity
  • Try zooming out and back in
  • Restart the app if map appears blank

📋 Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Why can't I see nearby bus stops?

Solution: Enable location permissions by going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Bustro and select "While Using App".

❓ Are the bus timings accurate?

Answer: Yes! Our data comes directly from Singapore's Land Transport Authority (LTA). Timing may vary slightly due to real-time traffic conditions.

❓ How do I add a bus stop to favorites?

Steps: Tap on any bus stop, then tap the heart icon. You can customize the name, icon, and color in your favorites list.

❓ What does the weather widget do?

Feature: The weather widget helps you plan your journey. Tap it to open the full Weather app for detailed forecasts.

❓ How do I get directions to a bus stop?

Instructions: On any bus stop details page, tap the distance/walking time to choose your preferred navigation app (Apple Maps, Google Maps, etc.).

🔧 Troubleshooting

App is crashing or slow

Try force-closing and restarting the app. If issues persist, restart your iPhone.

Bus timing not updating

Check your internet connection. Pull down to refresh the bus stop information.
